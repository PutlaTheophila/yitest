Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

Welcome to YI - What's On! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our app. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Registration: Users are registered by admins. We collect your name, mobile number, and profile picture when you use the app. We use OTP-based login for authentication.
  • Event Participation: When you RSVP or like an event, this interaction is stored to personalize your experience.
  • Coupons: If you redeem coupons, we store related activity to provide rewards and facilitate redemption with vendors.
  • Camera Access: We request camera access only for event hosts to scan participantsâ€™ QR codes for attendance purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To securely manage access through mobile number and OTP logins.
  • To enable you to RSVP and participate in YI events.
  • To allow event hosts to track attendance via QR code scan.
  • To process coupon requests and redemptions.
  • To personalize your profile and user experience in the app.

3. Data Security

We take security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information. All personal details are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data to any third-party persons or services. Your information is used strictly within the app to provide services as described above.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: Used only by event hosts to scan QR codes for verifying event attendance. The app does not access your photos or videos for any other purpose.
  • Profile Picture: Optionally uploaded by you to personalize your account. This is not shared outside the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as your account is active or as required to provide you with app services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting our support.

7. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for registered members only and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@pranaa.in.

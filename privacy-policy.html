  • To allow event hosts to track attendance via QR code scans within WhatsOn Yi.
  • To process coupon requests and redemptions in WhatsOn Yi.
  • To personalize your profile and user experience in the WhatsOn Yi app.

3. Data Security

We takes security seriously and implements appropriate measures to protect your information in WhatsOn Yi. All personal details are stored securely and are accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or disclose your data collected through WhatsOn Yi to any third-party persons or services. Your information is used strictly within the WhatsOn Yi app to provide services as described above.

5. Permissions

6. Data Retention

Pranaa Technologies retains your information in WhatsOn Yi only as long as your account is active or as required to provide you with app services. You may request deletion of your WhatsOn Yi account and associated data at any time by contacting our support team.

7. Children's Privacy

WhatsOn Yi is intended for registered members only and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Support & Account Management

Our team is here to help with anything related to YI - What's On. Please contact us via email for any queries, support needs, or account requests.

How to Contact Support

For support, please email us at:

admin@pranaa.in

When emailing, please include your registered mobile number (if applicable) and a detailed description of your query or issue for faster assistance.

Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:
admin@pranaa.in

Please use the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include your registered mobile number for identity confirmation.

We will process your account deletion request as required by app store policies and notify you when the deletion is complete.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my OTP login?
    Contact our support team from your registered email or include your registered mobile number.
  • How is my data protected?
    Read our Privacy Policy for details on data security.
  • Who can see my profile?
    Only authorized YI members and admins in the app. Your data is not shared externally.
